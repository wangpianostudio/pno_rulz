Top food near campus (not comprehensive, VERY subjective):

Koco Table (good Korean food, especially the budae jjigae)
Kung Fu Tea (best bubble tea on a budget, the side menu's onigiri are super good)
Happy Lemon (best bubble tea, kinda pricey)
Poco Ono Poke (great poke, great atmosphere inside)
Giordano's (fantastic pizza, especially the deep dish)
Ovo Frito Cafe (amazing brunch, great atmosphere inside)
Insomnia Cookies (great cookies, has a lot of vegan options, is open suuuuper late at night)
Joy Yee (huge selection of Asian food and bubble teas, has big servings, good for family-style)
10Q (best Korean barbeque)
Todoroki (best deal for all-you-can-eat sushi)
Kansaku (best Japanese food)

Top food on campus:


Close to Bienen:
Mod's (personal pizzas and salads, has dairy-free cheese, extremely customizable)
Wildcat Deli (ice cream pints and bars, sushi, poke, and energy/protein bars)
District Market (best sandwiches, 100% customizable, has dairy-free cheese, can be toasted)
Starbucks (drinks so sweet that they're practically dessert in a cup, popular study spot)
Other:
Lisa's (District Market-like convenience store, solid fast food- but has unreliable grill)
Fran's (popular nighttime hang-out spot, virtually same fast food as Lisa's but has milkshakes)

Top study spots on campus:

Deering Library (makes you feel super smart and fancy)
Info Comms (in Main Library) (loads of computers, some with pro Sony headphones, spinny chairs)
Periodicals (in Main Library) (best for crackdown time)
Shepard Hall Basement (HAS EGG CHAIRS!!! kitchens, big classrooms, and small glass cubbies for studying)
Allison 1st Floor Common Area (usually pretty empty, has view overlooking the walkway, quiet and peaceful)
Regenstein Common Area (beautiful lake view during day, usually noisy- bring headphones and you'll b ok)
